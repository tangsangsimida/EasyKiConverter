# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** March 13, 2026

English Version | [中文版本](privacy_policy/)

## 1. Introduction
EasyKiConverter ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our desktop application.

## 2. Data Collection
**We do not collect, store, share, or transmit any personal data.**

- **Local Preferences Only**: The application saves certain user preferences (such as window position, theme settings, or last used options) strictly on your local device. These files are stored locally and are never transmitted to us or any third party.
- **No Personal Information**: We do not collect names, email addresses, location data, device identifiers, or any other personally identifiable information.
- **No Analytics**: We do not use any third-party analytics or advertising services.

## 3. Internet Usage
The application may access the internet solely to fetch public component preview images from LCSC (Jialichuang) servers based on part numbers entered by the user. No user-specific data is sent during this process.

## 4. Third-Party Services
Our app is open-source software licensed under GPL-3.0. We do not integrate any third-party SDKs that collect user data.

## 5. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
